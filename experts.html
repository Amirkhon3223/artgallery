Ana Loktionova

Organizer, curator, artivist Ana Loktionova is a curator, organiser, and artivist based in Minsk who is interested in locally focused, accessible and inclusive approaches to spaces that can resist the neoliberal growth-driven structures they find themselves in. Her attention currently spikes for explorations of spaces, self-care and horizontal communication. She is co-founder of the festival of queer culture DOTYK (2015), ran the gallery canteena.xyz (2016-2019) and member of Ambasada Kultury (2020). Ana is a co-founder of Chascha - art-retreat program for ac(r)tivists in Belarus.
